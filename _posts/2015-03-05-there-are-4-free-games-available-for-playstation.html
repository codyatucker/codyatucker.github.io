Earlier today, in the aftermath of another winter storm, I thought about hopping on PSN and buying a new game. But, with a new car payment on the way, I decided to save my money.

Later, I decided to check out the Playstation Store on my PS4 - just in case there were any deals around.

Turns out, there are actually four games available for free to PS+ subscribers: OlliOlli 2, Oddworld: Abe’s Oddysee (New ‘n’ Tasty edition), Valiant Hearts - The Great War, and Counterspy. As some folks at Reddit have pointed out, this is awesome.

You can argue as much as you want about the drawbacks of buying software games, or about how you in some way pay for the games with the PS+ subscription fee. The value of getting $75 worth of games for $0 extra can not be debated.
