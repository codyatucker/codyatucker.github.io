I haven’t used Path for months, but after seeing it’s iOS 6 update, I decided to give it a go.

The newest version is really good. Most apps like Path are plagued by a weird jittery scrolling issue when passing pictures in the feed.

Path’s latest version could be the nicest, smoothest social networking app available. If you haven’t used it in a while, it’s now time to revisit.
