David Sparks:

So my host calls in the IT guy. He walked in, looked at my set up and said it:

“Oh. You’re Mac”

Those were his exact words. He delivered them dripping with judgment. He didn’t say I was using a Mac. He said I was Mac.

In my previous job, the IT department dealt with Macs, iPhones and iPads. Still, there was always a sense of cynicism about them.

At the same time, some of the IT workers enjoyed using their cheaper Nooks and Kindle Fire’s, all while looking forward to using the original Microsoft Surface.
