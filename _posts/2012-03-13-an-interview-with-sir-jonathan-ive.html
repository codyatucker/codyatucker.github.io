The London Evening Standard scored an interview with Sir Jonathan Ive, and it is predictably filled with quotable quotes. Like this:

Our goals are very simple - to design and make better products. If we can’t make something that is better, we won’t do it.

and this:

Committees just don’t work, and it’s not about price, schedule or a bizarre marketing goal to appear different - they are corporate goals with scant regard for people who use the product.
