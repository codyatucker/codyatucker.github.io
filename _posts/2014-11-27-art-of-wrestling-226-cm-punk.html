Even if you’re not a wrestling fan, this is a must listen.

CM Punk breaks his silence over why he left the WWE earlier this year, and the details are earth shattering. This will definitely blow your mind if your a wrestling fan, but you’ll certainly be taken aback even if you’ve never watched two men lock up in the squared circle.

Punk, it appears, leaves no stone unturned, and while this is all his perspective, there’s currently no reason to believe it isn’t true.

I would argue that this could very well be a landmark moment for the podcasting medium, given the potential fallout inside the WWE.

Also: Kudos for Punk doing it all for free on a podcast. He easily could have made millions off this story, be it in a book or a high-profile interview.
