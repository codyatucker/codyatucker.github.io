Circa:

“The Justice Dept. will not bring civil rights charges against former officer Darren Wilson, according to anonymous law enforcement officials cited by the New York Times and the Washington Post. A broader Justice Dept. civil rights investigation into the policing practices of the Ferguson Police Department remains open.”

It’s hard to make a civil rights case here, since he was likely looking for a robbery suspect whose description fit Brown’s. I think there is, however, still a case against Wilson’s handling of the situation.
