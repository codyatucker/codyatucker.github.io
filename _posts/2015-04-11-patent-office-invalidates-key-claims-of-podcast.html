--- layout: post title: Patent Office invalidates key claims of 'podcast patent' date: '2015-04-11T23:33:19-05:00' tags: - podcast - podcasting - patent tumblr_url: http://codytucker.tumblr.com/post/116174993144/patent-office-invalidates-key-claims-of-podcast --- Patent Office invalidates key claims of 'podcast patent'

The U.S. Patent and Trademark Office on April 10 invalidated several key claims of a patent that was used to sue several well-known podcasters. The invalidation means that the so-called “podcasting patent” can no longer be used to sue podcasters. The Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF), an advocacy group, had petitioned against the patent.

That’s a win.
