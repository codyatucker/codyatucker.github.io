Rob Beschizza:

After a presentation that dragged on for hours, Sony failed to show the assembled game press the gadget they were there to see.

My only hope is that, since this is 2013, the hardware of the device simply doesn’t matter. All that matters then, is the experience; the feel of the controller and the quality of the service.

But if that were the case, Sony would have spared the press on tech specs, and saved that useless information for the developers at a later date.
