Circa:

A total of 7.9 million vinyl records were sold through early December, according to Nielsen. That represents year-over-year growth of 49%. Jack White’s “Lazaretto” was the best-selling record of the year, with 75,700 units sold.

“Lazaretto” is a fantastic album. Also, I should get my record player fixed.
