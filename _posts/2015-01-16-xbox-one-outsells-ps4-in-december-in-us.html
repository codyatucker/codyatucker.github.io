Circa:

The Xbox One outsold the PlayStation 4 in the U.S. for the second month in a row in December 2014, according to the research firm NPD. Microsoft did not say how many units it shipped but claimed it was a record-breaking holiday season for Xbox.

Sony still claims the title for worldwide sales.
