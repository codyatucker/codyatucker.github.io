California voters have passed Proposition 47, the Associated Press has projected. That makes California the first state in the nation to “de-felonize” all drug use. Prop 47 reclassifies the possession of heroin, methamphetamine and other illegal drugs, and theft of $950 or less, as misdemeanors in the state.
