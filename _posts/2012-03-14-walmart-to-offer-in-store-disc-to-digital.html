The only people I can see having any interest in this are those with large DVD collections. I don’t have a very large collection, but if I did, I wouldn’t even consider dragging them all to Walmart (of all places) and have them converted for $2-$5 a pop.

Consider the “used buy back” market: People bring drums of DVDs that they longer watch to sell or trade in for cash or store credit. Asking people to pay to convert movies that they likely have no intention of ever seeing again seems like a pretty hard sell.
