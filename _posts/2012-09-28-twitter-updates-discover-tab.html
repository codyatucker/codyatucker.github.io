Twitter Blog:

Now we’re improving Discover again: when you visit the Discover tab on twitter.com, you’ll see a continuous stream of Tweets, which automatically expand to show you the most relevant stories and most engaging photos.

…on their website.

Wouldn’t it be better for Twitter, a company built on mobile, to launch new features simultaneously across platforms?

Sure it may be easier to push updates to their site, but they create a frustrating amount of fragmentation when they do this.
