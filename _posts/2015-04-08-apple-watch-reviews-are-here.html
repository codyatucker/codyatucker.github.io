I’m bummed that the embargo fell at 8 a.m., instead of the usual midnight PST. That means I missed the initial review onslaught by a couple of hours. I sleep too much.

Anyways, Mashable is compiling a nifty cheat sheet as the reviews come out. In a perfect world, we would read every word of every review. It’s just too bad we don’t have the time.

The reviews, so far, are appropriately mixed. John Gruber’s review is a great place to start. It seems pretty fair, and a good indication of just how new and unique this device is.

Otherwise, it should be no surprise that the Apple Watch is polarizing at launch. Such has been the case with every new product that Apple has ever released.
