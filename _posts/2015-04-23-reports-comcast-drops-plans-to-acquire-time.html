Circa:

Bloomberg, Comcast owned CNBC and the New York Times reported April 23 that Comcast has dropped its plans to acquire Time Warner Cable amid regulatory resistance. Both stories were based on unnamed sources, with Bloomberg suggesting an official announcement could be made by tomorrow.
