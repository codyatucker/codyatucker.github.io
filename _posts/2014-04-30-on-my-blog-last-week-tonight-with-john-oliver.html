HBO was (apparently) kind enough to allow the first episode of John Oliver’s new show, “Last Week Tonight,” to stream on YouTube.

That means, you get to watch an all-new HBO show for free.

I watched it under my current, excruciatingly expensive cable plan, and it was worth every penny. Thus, I would argue that you owe HBO money for letting you watch it for free.
