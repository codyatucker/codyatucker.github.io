Dave Caolo makes an excellent point:

Imagine that Apple isn’t specifically designing a watch, but the next step in its portable devices. Something that does much of what iOS devices do today, only in a novel way, and is smaller than current devices. Small enough to be, say, attached to one’s wrist.

Another case where perspective makes all the difference.
